Beginner Plant Care Tips

💧 Do Not Overwater

More indoor plants die from overwatering than anything else! Stick your finger an inch into the soil—if it feels damp, wait a few more days.

☀️ Light Matters

Match your plant to your window spaces. Most beginner varieties prefer bright, indirect sunlight rather than harsh, direct midday heat.

🪴 Use Drainage Holes

Stagnant water trapped at the bottom of a pot causes root rot. Always make sure your interior pots let excess water flow out safely.

Beginner Plant Care Tips

💧 Do Not Overwater

More indoor plants die from overwatering than underwatering! Always check the soil. Stick your finger an inch deep—if it feels dry, it's time to water. If it's wet, wait a few days.

☀️ Choose the Right Light

Match your plant to your window spaces. Most beginner plants prefer bright, indirect sunlight. Avoid placing delicate leaves in harsh, direct midday sun.

🪴 Use Pots with Drainage

Stagnant water at the bottom of a pot causes root rot. Always make sure your plant pots have holes at the bottom to let excess water escape safely.
